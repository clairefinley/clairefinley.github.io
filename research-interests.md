## Research Interests

My research centers on observational and computational astrophysics, with emphasis on:

- Galaxy evolution across cosmic time
- Stellar populations and star-formation histories
- Gas, dust, and feedback in galaxy ecosystems
- Connections between galaxies and their large-scale environments

## Current Focus

I am currently developing projects that combine survey data analysis and physically motivated modeling to better constrain the processes that shape galaxy growth.

## Methods

- Multi-wavelength data analysis
- Statistical inference and modeling
- Reproducible computational workflows
