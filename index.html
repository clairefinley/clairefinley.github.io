About Me

Hi, I'm Claire Finley , an astronomy PhD student at The University of Texas at Austin working with Prof. Brendan P. Bowler (UC Santa Barbara). My research focuses on how planetary systems form and evolve, with an emphasis on wide-orbit, directly imaged exoplanets and their circumplanetary disks.

I'm currently leading a multiwavelength photometric program to characterize the accretion and circumplanetary disk properties of directly-imaged exoplanets using the Hubble Space Telescope and James Webb Space Telescope.

If you're interested in my work or potential collaborations, I'd love to connect! Please feel free to reach out via email or check out my CV linked below.
